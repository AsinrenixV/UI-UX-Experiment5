# UI-UX-Experiment5

## Aim:
To design and develop user-centered UI prototypes for Zoom and Google Class room App website to identify usability issues and improve user experience.

## Algorithm:


1. The system combines the features of Zoom and Google Classroom to create an integrated online learning platform.  
2. Users register and log in as Teacher or Student using secure authentication.  
3. Teachers create virtual classrooms similar to Google Classroom for managing subjects, students, assignments, and study materials.  
4. The system generates unique classroom codes for students to join the classes.  
5. Teachers schedule and conduct live video classes using Zoom-like meeting features such as video, audio, screen sharing, and chat.  
6. Students join live classes through meeting links or classroom dashboards.  
7. Teachers upload assignments, notes, PDFs, and recorded sessions for students to access anytime.  
8. Students submit assignments, attend quizzes, and interact with teachers through chat and discussion forums.   
9. The system automatically tracks attendance, assignment completion, and student performance analytics.  
10. Notifications and reminders are sent for upcoming classes, deadlines, and announcements, creating a complete smart learning environment.  



## Output:

### GOOGLE CLASS ROOM:

Google Classroom helps teachers create and manage virtual classrooms easily.
It allows sharing assignments, notes, PDFs, and study materials with students online.
Students can submit homework, attend quizzes, and receive grades digitally.
The platform also supports announcements, communication, and organized learning management.

### ZOOM APP:


Zoom allows users to conduct live video meetings and online classes.
It supports audio/video calling, screen sharing, chat, and meeting recording features.
Multiple participants can join meetings through links or meeting IDs from anywhere.
The app is widely used for online learning, business meetings, webinars, and virtual communication.

### EDUCLASS ROOM:
